Simo's Blackjack Rules

In a game of Blackjack your goal is to get as close to 21. You also have to bet the dealer's hand!. If your hand value goes over 21 you lose... Same is true for the dealer.

Select the bet

Select the bet size by clicking a chip of your liking. You only have 10000 chips so chose wisely.

DEAL

The initial cards will be dealt when 'DEAL' button is pressed.

HIT

Draw one card by pressing 'HIT' button. Be careful not to go over 21!

STAND

Pass the turn to dealer by pressing 'STAND' button and wait for the outcome.

BET

Once the hand is over press 'BET' button to continue playing.
